Burnout City

ABOUT THE EVENT

Burnout City is not just an event- it is a fully built city of experiences.

For one day, the venue transforms into a high-energy urban world inspired by cars, bikes, culture, and community. Every zone is intentionally designed, from drift arenas and runway showcases to lifestyle spaces and immersive brand districts.

Brands don’t just set up stalls - they become part of the city through dedicated districts with their own identity, interactions, and audience touchpoints. Even ticket categories are mapped as locations within the city, making every tier feel like a place, not a pass.

Guided by street-style signages, named lanes, and branded intersections, Burnout City becomes a world built to explore - where performance, design, and culture come together in their most ideal form.

This is not something you attend.
This is a city you enter.
